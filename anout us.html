Midori: Green Guide and Carbon Footprint Tracker Midori is your ultimate companion in the journey towards a greener lifestyle. This innovative app helps you calculate and track your carbon emissions, providing you with insights and actionable steps to reduce your environmental impact. Key Features:
Carbon Footprint Calculator: Easily input your daily activities and get an accurate estimate of your carbon emissions. Track your progress over time and see how small changes can make a big difference.
Gamified Learning: Learn about sustainability and eco-friendly practices through engaging and interactive challenges. Earn rewards and badges as you complete tasks and improve your carbon footprint.
Social Connectivity: Connect with friends and family within the app. Share your progress, compete in eco-challenges, and motivate each other to live more sustainably.<> Chatbot Assistance: Meet Midori, your friendly chatbot guide. Midori is here to answer all your questions about carbon emissions, provide tips on reducing your footprint, and support you throughout your green journey..
